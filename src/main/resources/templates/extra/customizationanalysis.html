Customization Analysis

Visualizing product customizations in Software Product Lines by means of Alluvial diagrams

A brief before you start!

Two views will be provided:



The position map

The position map represents the possible diffs (granularity) that can be consulted. It also keeps track of the consulted diffs (in blue) and currently consulted view (in orange).

The Diagram view

We resort to alluvial diagrams to represent customization effort. On the left hand-side PL assets are arranged, while in the right hand-side products' assets. Links between both represent the customization effort.
